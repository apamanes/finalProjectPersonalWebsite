Appeal

This rule states that all characters should have some level of charisma. In other words, they should look in a way that looks interesting to the viewer. It shouldn't apply to only the hero of the story but also the villain. In every chacarter for an animation, there should be appeal in each of them.

Take a look at Mickey Mouse for example. There are so many features that make him an appealing chacarter, from the gloves, the shorts, and his signature ears. All these features has made him a well-known character around the world for so many years. If you can design a character as unique but different as him, you can come up with amazing characters.
